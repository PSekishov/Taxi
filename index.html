A little bit about our history

Company was founded in 1978 by local cab drivers. It is one the oldest cab companies in Portland. Even though we are not one of the largest cab companies, we have stayed prosperous in business by offering our clients a variety of options that other cab companies don't, such as: reservations as far off ahead of time as you like, confirmation of trips, email receipts through SQUARE, unbeatable discounts and flat rates. We have always been and still are happy to establish company contract agreements in order to transport company employees and medical transports all over the city.

And these are some of our goals for the future

We want to jump in on the environmentally friendly vehicles plan and hopefully soon we will add even more hybrid cars to our fleet and also add electric cars that run on pure electricity. We are feverishly and optimistically working on this project and many others. We want to run our business with more efficiency and more modern technology. We will do our best to keep you posted about our progress.
